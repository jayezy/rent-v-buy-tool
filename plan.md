# Rent vs Buy Tool — Plan

## Overview
A financial comparison tool that helps users decide whether renting or buying a home is more cost-effective based on their personal financial situation.

## Core Features
- [ ] Input form for financial parameters (home price, rent, down payment, interest rate, etc.)
- [ ] Side-by-side cost comparison over a configurable time horizon
- [ ] Visualization of cumulative costs (rent vs buy) over time
- [ ] Breakeven analysis — when does buying become cheaper than renting?
- [ ] Summary with recommendation

## Key Inputs
- Home purchase price
- Down payment (% or $)
- Mortgage interest rate
- Mortgage term (15/30 years)
- Monthly rent
- Annual rent increase (%)
- Property tax rate
- Homeowner's insurance
- HOA fees
- Maintenance costs (% of home value)
- Home appreciation rate
- Investment return rate (opportunity cost of down payment)
- Tax filing status / marginal tax rate

## Key Outputs
- Monthly and total cost of renting over N years
- Monthly and total cost of buying over N years (mortgage, taxes, insurance, maintenance, HOA)
- Equity built over time
- Opportunity cost of down payment
- Net cost comparison
- Breakeven year
- Charts / graphs
